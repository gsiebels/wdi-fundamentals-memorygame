Instructions

Concentrations, also known as Match Match, Memory,Pelmanism,Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in whitch all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

This is a fun game that will give you hours of entertainment, but it doesn't end there... it will also improve your memory and it's a great GYM for your brain!
